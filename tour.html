Source of the Nile

Uganda Museum

Uganda is blessed with more than 3 World Heritage Sites and a diverse range of tourist attractions. These range from historial, cultural sites to wildlife in National parks and beyond. It is also the home to the Source of the River Nile and also Lake Victoria the largest freshwater lake in the world.

Uganda is lucky to have half of the overall population of mountain gorillas and in two national parks - Bwindi and Mgahinga. Mountain gorillas are endangered but their numbers have grown to slightly above 1000 in the last decade.
